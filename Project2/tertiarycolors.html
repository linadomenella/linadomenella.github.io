Finally, you have the Tertiary Colors which is made up of six different colors, yellow-green, blue-green, blue-violet, red-violet, red-orange, and yellow-orange. These are the Tertiary Colors because they are the colors that result when you mix the primary and secondary colors together, as you saw on the last page.
